0 A.D.

About

0 A.D. is a free, open-source Real Time Strategy game which is under development by Wildfire Games. You will play as the leader of an ancient civilization, who must gather resources in order to raise a military force and dominate your enemies. Check out the Official 0 A.D. website for more information.

    Highlights

  • Emphasis on Historical Accuracy while still being playable and enjoyable
  • Free of Charge - always has been, always will be
  • Free as in "Freedom" - freely licensed software
  • Still under development
